Barbecue Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 85g brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp malt vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato puré

Free Recipe Book

Method

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a saucepan and add the onion. Cook over a gentle
  2. Heat 4-5 mins, until softened. Add the remaining ingredients,
  3. Season and mix. Bring to the boil, then reduce heat and
  4. Dimmer for 20-30 mins, until thickened. For a smooth sauce,
  5. Dimply whizz the mixture in a food processor or with a hand
  6. Blender for a few seconds.

Enjoy!
